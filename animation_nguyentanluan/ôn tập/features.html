Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our features

How easy is it to migrate from another platform?

We provide a seamless migration process with dedicated support. Our team will help you export your data from your current platform and import it into TechStart. Most migrations are completed within 24-48 hours.

Can I customize the dashboard to my needs?

Yes! Our dashboard is fully customizable. You can add, remove, or rearrange widgets to create a personalized view that shows exactly the metrics you care about. We also offer custom widget development for enterprise clients.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer 24/7 customer support via chat, email, and phone. All plans include access to our knowledge base and video tutorials. Enterprise plans come with a dedicated account manager and priority support.

Is there a limit to the number of users?

The Starter plan includes up to 5 users. The Pro plan includes up to 20 users. For Enterprise plans, there's no user limit - you can add as many team members as you need.
